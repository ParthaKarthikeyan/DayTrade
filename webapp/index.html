This is a simulator. It runs the exact Opening Range Breakout strategy and risk rules in your browser on one day of 1-minute data — no install, no server. Stop is the tighter of the opposite opening-range edge or your max-stop %; winners scale out (breakeven at +1R, half off at +2R, the runner on a 0.5% trailing stop); the daily loss cap halts trading for the day. For live automated trading you still need an always-on host — a phone browser tab can't run all session, and broker keys don't belong in client-side code.
